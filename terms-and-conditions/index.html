2. Eligibility Requirements

To undertake CBT training you must:

  • Hold a valid Great Britain or Northern Ireland provisional driving or motorcycle licence.
  • Be at least 16 years of age (for mopeds up to 50cc) or 17 years of age (for motorcycles up to 125cc).
  • Be able to read a number plate from a distance of 20 metres (with glasses or contact lenses if normally worn).
  • Declare any medical conditions or disabilities that may affect your ability to ride safely. We may ask for written confirmation from a medical professional in some cases.

For A1, A2, and Direct Access Scheme licence training, additional eligibility criteria apply as set by the DVSA. It is your responsibility to verify your eligibility before booking. We are not liable for any booking fees paid by students who are ineligible to sit a test.
