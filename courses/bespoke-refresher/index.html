Flexible Training

Bespoke & Refresher Courses

Not every rider fits the same mould. We offer a range of flexible options — from nervous beginners to experienced riders returning to the saddle.

Our Options

Find the Right Course for You

Whether you're trying motorcycling for the first time or brushing up skills you haven't used in years, we have something that fits.

Bespoke Taster

Perfect for those who've never ridden before — nervous riders, or anyone who wants to try before committing to a full course. Get a feel for the bike in a safe, relaxed off-road setting.

Gearing Up for Gears

Been riding automatics and ready to tackle a geared motorcycle? Our specialised one-hour session teaches you the ins and outs of clutch control and gear changes. Ideal for 17-year-olds who started on a 50cc scooter at 16. Bring your CBT certificate and both parts of your licence.

Hourly Lessons

Completed your CBT but can't fit in a full day? We offer flexible two-hour lessons to work around your schedule. Use your own bike or one of ours — we've got you covered.

Refresher Sessions

Already licensed but lacking in confidence? Perhaps it's been a while since you've been on two wheels and you're eyeing that Harley, V-Max, or GSX-R. Our refresher sessions are designed to help you regain control and get back to riding with confidence.

A2 Full Licence (Under 24s)

If you're under 24 and aiming for a full licence, the A2 is your pathway. This licence authorises you to ride motorcycles up to 47bhp — a great stepping stone to an unrestricted licence.

Accelerated Access

Held your A2 licence for two years and ready to go unrestricted? Let us help you train for your full, unrestricted licence. You're closer than you think — get in touch to discuss options.

Failed a Module?

Don't be disheartened — module failures are common. Book with us and we'll work through the specific areas you need to improve, guiding you confidently towards a pass.

Let's Talk

Not Sure Which Course Is Right for You?

Get in touch and we'll help you find the best option for your experience level and goals.
