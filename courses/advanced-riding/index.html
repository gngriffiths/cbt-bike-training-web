About the Course

The Enhanced Rider Scheme

The Enhanced Rider Scheme (ERS) is a DVSA-backed post-test training programme designed to make already-qualified riders safer, more confident, and more skilled on roads across Brighton, Brighton and Hove, and Sussex.

If you're a category A or A2 rider looking to elevate your skills and earn the esteemed Enhanced Rider Scheme certificate, we've got you covered. Obtaining the ERS certificate will not only boost your confidence and proficiency on the road but also make you eligible for insurance discounts with nearly 30 different providers — including major names like Carole Nash and Devitts.

Our ERS training sessions are led by a DVSA-Approved Post-Test Motorcycle Trainer and last a minimum of one full day, tailored to your current ability and riding goals.

What You Gain

Benefits of ERS Training

Insurance Discounts

Your ERS certificate makes you eligible for insurance discounts with nearly 30 providers, including Carole Nash and Devitts — savings that can easily offset the cost of training.

DVSA Certificate of Competence

Upon completion you'll receive a detailed personal report along with the official DVSA Certificate of Competence – Enhanced Rider Bonus, recognised nationwide.

Expert Instruction

Training is delivered by a DVSA-Approved Post-Test Motorcycle Trainer over a minimum of one full day — focused, personalised, and tailored to your riding style and goals.

On Completion

What You'll Receive

Detailed Personal Report

A comprehensive written assessment of your riding, highlighting strengths and areas for continued development — a valuable document for any rider.

DVSA Certificate of Competence

The official Enhanced Rider Bonus certificate entitles you to applicable insurance discounts and demonstrates your commitment to safe, skilled motorcycling.
