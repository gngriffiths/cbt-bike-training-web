Getting Started

New Rider Courses

Whether you're 16 or 60, we offer a safe, pressure-free introduction to motorcycling — in a secure off-road environment with a DVSA-certified instructor.

Our Courses

Two Options for First-Time Riders

Both courses are conducted in a secure off-road area and are designed to build confidence gradually, without any pressure to perform.

New Rider

Ages 17 and above

A one-hour lesson in our secure off-road setting, guided by a certified DVSA instructor. Ideal for complete beginners — you'll learn the fundamentals of controlling a manual motorcycle, including starting, stopping, gear changes, and basic manoeuvres like figure-of-eights and U-turns.

The course fee includes motorcycle or scooter hire and all safety equipment.

Young Rider

Age 16 — first step onto two wheels

A one-hour introductory lesson for 16-year-olds, conducted in our secure off-road area on a 50cc automatic scooter. Designed to give young beginners an optimum start before progressing to CBT.

Our fleet for young riders consists of automatic 50cc mopeds only (no manual clutch or gears).

Good to Know

Course Details

Prerequisites

Before enrolling on any of our courses, you should be a competent cyclist — able to ride a bicycle confidently, demonstrating good balance and control. This forms the essential foundation for safe motorcycle training.

What's Included

Your course fee covers motorcycle or scooter hire, and all necessary safety equipment including helmet and protective clothing. You just need to arrive with appropriate footwear and leg wear.

Duration

Each lesson is approximately one hour, but training duration is tailored to individual progress. Your instructor will assess your ability and advise on how much training is right for you — there's never any pressure to rush.

Your Journey

What Comes Next?

The New Rider and Young Rider courses are the perfect first step before taking your CBT. Once you've built confidence here, you'll be well prepared for the full CBT course.
