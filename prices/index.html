Transparent Pricing

Prices

No hidden fees. No surprises. All course prices include the use of a training bike, equipment and practical test fees where applicable.

New Rider

One hour on the bike with tuition in a safe, private off-road environment.

  • One person £40
  • Two people booked together £70

Gearing Up for Gears

An introductory session covering clutch control and gear changes for riders new to manual bikes.

  • One hour session £40

Compulsory Basic Training (CBT)

A full-day training course with a maximum of two riders per instructor. Includes bike hire, insurance, equipment and petrol.

  • Standard CBT (bike, equipment & insurance included) £195
  • CBT Renewal — using your own bike £150
  • CBT Retest (full day) £120
  • 2-hour road ride (if not successful first time) £60
  • 1:1 CBT — bike hire, insurance, equipment & petrol included £250

Full Licence — A1, A2 & Direct Access (DAS)

Available for A1 (17+), A2 (19+) and full A licence (24+). Module 1 & 2 tests are taken on the final day, or split between the last two days. All packages include bike, equipment and test fees.

  • Three-Day Course — £770

    Ideal for the experienced rider looking to upgrade their licence — for example from A2 to full A. Module 1 & 2 tests on the final day.

  • Four-Day Course — £870

    Our most popular course. Suited to riders who have completed their CBT and feel comfortable on a bike. Module 1 & 2 tests on the final day.

    Most Popular
  • Five-Day Course — £970

    Designed for those new to biking who want to feel fully safe and confident before testing. Module 1 & 2 tests on the final day.

  • Full day training — 5 hours (not part of a course) £250
  • Recap session + accompanied test From £100 + test fee

Refresher Course

  • All inclusive — bike hire, insurance, equipment & fuel £220

Bespoke Lesson

  • CBT £60/hr
  • DAS (big bike) £90/hr
